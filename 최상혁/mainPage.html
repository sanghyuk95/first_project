2022 MEN's Fall

So, a new and fresh idea of male beauty and masculinity, then, and one that clearly resonates with where we are today. But in honing his vision of manhood in 2022, Vaccarello also went back a hundred years or so, to the 1920s. It’s there in the often rounded, softer, more wrapped silhouettes he explored, but also with the look of his models, who have the coiffured and preening elegance of the era’s silent movie male stars; their exotic, chiseled, androgynous look was radical and revolutionary at the time.
